Our Story

A Legacy of Grand Celebrations

Nestled on the Outer Ring Road, Hudkeshwar, Krishnam Lawns & Banquets has been Nagpur's premier destination for weddings, receptions, and grand events. We blend timeless elegance with modern luxury to create unforgettable moments.

Our sprawling venue and dedicated team ensure every detail — from décor to cuisine — is curated with perfection, making your special day truly extraordinary.
